COMPUTER ENGINEERING

History

Uşak University Computer Engineering Department is one of the departments that started its activities after the establishment of the Engineering Faculty in 1992. However, it remained as one of the departments without faculty members for many years. In June 2021, one Associate Professor Doctor and one Lecturer Doctor joined the department staff and joined the departments of the Faculty of Engineering with teaching staff. Since June 2021, studies related to the department have started.

About Us

What is Computer Engineering: Computer engineering is an engineering department that examines concepts such as software, hardware, operating systems, computer networks in the finest detail, finds solutions to problems arising in these areas, and produces new systems and programmes. What does a Computer Engineer do? The field of computer engineering has a very large job range. People who graduate from this department are active in many fields such as hardware engineering, software engineering, robotics engineering, database expertise, web design expertise, network internet expertise, control engineering, system engineering, test engineering, access engineering, network engineering, network engineering, data analyst. About Our Department The Department of Computer Engineering has become one of the departments of our Faculty with faculty members with the addition of one Associate Professor Doctor and one Lecturer Doctor to the department in 2021.

Vision and Mission

Vision It has been determined as a department that educates the competent engineers needed by the industry, academia and society both nationally and internationally, and educates its students as engineers and scientists who will have a say in the future of the sector. Mission To be a department that conducts qualified research at the universal level and does not compromise on quality, can apply and transfer their knowledge, skills and experience in a good way, can think analytically, is open to innovations, has lifelong learning and teamwork skills, has a sense of ethical responsibility towards the profession and society, and educates engineers and scientists who will have a say in the future of the sector.

FOOD ENGINEERING

About Us

Our food engineering department started its education and training activities in the autumn semester of 2015-2016 academic year with a total of 120 students, 60 students in regular education and 60 students in secondary education. In order to carry out both education and training and scientific studies within our department, 4 laboratories, 2 100 m2 and 2 70 m2, were established with their interior furnishings. These laboratories are named as Instrumental Analysis, Food Technology Laboratory, Biotechnology and Food Microbiology Laboratory. Our laboratories are equipped with devices for basic education and research purposes such as capillary electrophoresis, high pressure liquid chromatography, automatic oil extraction device, protein determination device, colour determination device and various ovens and incubators. In order for our students to have a good technical knowledge and to develop themselves socially, our Food Engineering education programme is prepared according to MÜDEK standards and has a rich pool of courses. In addition, our department has made a good start in terms of scientific research. Although our department is a newly opened department within the faculty, our faculty members are currently conducting 1 TÜBİTAK Project and 3 BAP projects. 1 TUBITAK project was accepted as an interdisciplinary project to be carried out in our department in May. Our faculty members published 4 international articles within the scope of SCI, 1 Turkish article and many papers in various congresses between January-June 2015. Our students who graduate from the Department of Food Engineering receive the title of FOOD ENGINEER. Food Engineers, who can work in all kinds of enterprises that process foodstuffs, do not have difficulty in finding a job with the increase in the number of large food producing establishments in our country in recent years and the legal obligation of food engineers to work in such establishments. Our graduates work as Production Engineer, Analyser, Expert, Expertise, Product Manager, Sales Engineer and Researcher in Production, Quality Control, Project, Marketing, Consultancy, R&D departments of Ministry of Food, Agriculture and Livestock, Research, Education, Extension and Control Institutions, TUBITAK, Ministry of Health, Public Health Laboratories, State Planning Organisation, Turkish Standards Institute, Municipalities, Private Institutions. As Uşak University Food Engineering Department, in accordance with the developing socio-economic conditions of our country, Muratbey Gıda, which operates in Uşak province, has been negotiated with Muratbey Gıda and it has been agreed that the first 10 students who are successful in our department will be provided with internship and guaranteed employment after internship. Since our department is newly established, our first goal in the future is to ensure the development of education and research laboratories so that studies can be carried out to provide master's and doctoral education in addition to undergraduate education. In addition, in order to improve the practical skills of our students during their education period and to gain experience, the establishment of a pilot enterprise for different food industry branches within Uşak University Food Engineering is among our future work plans.

CIVIL ENGINEERING

General Information

Engineering is the art of organising and managing people and controlling materials and power in nature. Based on this definition, civil engineering is one of the most fundamental branches of engineering. What do civil engineers do? Civil engineers take the material in nature, put it into a certain form and put it to use in a way that is beneficial to humanity. Contrary to popular belief, civil engineers do not only build buildings. They take part in every aspect of the zoning and construction of countries. Some of these are railway, highway, bridge, dam, power plant, factory, industrial plant, treatment plant, port, airport, business centre. About Our Department Department of Civil Engineering was established in 2013. There are regular education and graduate programmes. Academic Year An academic year consists of fourteen-week autumn and spring semesters. The duration of undergraduate education is eight semesters (four years). Language of Instruction The medium of instruction at Uşak University is Turkish. There is also an optional English Preparatory Programme. Registration and Counselling Services While the registration procedures of the students who will start our university are carried out on the dates determined by ÖSYM, the registration renewal procedures for the students in the intermediate classes are carried out online between the dates announced by our university, under the knowledge and supervision of the Department of Student Affairs and the counsellor lecturers established in the students' own units. Assessment and Evaluation Graduation and success in courses are determined according to the credit system. Exams consist of midterm and final exams. At least one midterm exam is held for each course. The method of pure evaluation and relative evaluation is applied in the success grade. The values obtained are converted into letter grades through Uşak University Student Automation System. The student has to take one midterm and one final exam in a semester. During the evaluation, 40% of the student's midterm exams, homework, attendance and laboratory studies during the semester and 60% of the final exam are taken and a general evaluation is made. Horizontal Transfer Applications for transfer between higher education institutions applying equivalent education programmes are made at the beginning of the academic year in higher education institutions applying the year system, at the beginning of each semester in higher education institutions applying the semester system, and applications for transfer before the start of the courses are made with the decision of the board of directors of faculties and colleges. Vertical Transfer It is a programme that enables successful students who graduated from Vocational Schools and open education associate degree programmes to be placed in formal education. In this programme, all vertical transfers, except for the Open Education Faculty, are carried out by a vertical transfer exam organised by ÖSYM. Double Major - Minor Programmes With the Double Major programme, students who successfully complete their major undergraduate programmes are also provided with the opportunity to study for a second undergraduate degree. With the minor programme, students who successfully complete their major undergraduate programmes are provided with the opportunity to take courses from the second undergraduate programme they are interested in, thereby increasing their knowledge and experience and obtaining a certificate. Graduation Requirements Students who successfully complete the education programme, have no failed courses and have a GPA of at least 2.00 and 240 credits (ECTS) are eligible to graduate. The student's status is examined by the counsellor and the head of the department and the graduation decision is taken by the unit board of directors. In order for the students of our department to graduate, they are required to complete Construction Site (20 days) and Office (20 days) internships in the 2nd and 3rd year summer terms.

History

Department of Civil Engineering was established in 2013. There are regular education, second education and graduate programmes.

Vision and Mission

The features that we have adopted as a mission as a department and we want our students to gain; To have technical knowledge and equipment, To be able to produce rational and mathematical solutions to engineering problems, To be an individual who follows current developments, researches, questions and presents ideas, To apply the professional knowledge gained in the awareness of ethical and professional responsibility, To provide the best benefit to the society as a civil engineer who is prone to teamwork, capable of design, experiment and analysis. Our first goal is to provide the society with civil engineers who are exemplary with their knowledge and practical solutions, first in Turkey and then all over the world.

Mission and Vision

Mission Our department is committed to educating textile engineers who are open to innovation and continuous development, can foresee the future, are prone to multidisciplinary studies, have the necessary solid infrastructure to identify and solve engineering problems, and are aware of their responsibilities towards humanity and the environment. Our main task is to make significant contributions to textile engineering education and literature with original researches at national and international level in order to meet the needs of the industry and society. Vision To be a reputable department that contributes to the development of textile engineering science and technology with original researches by taking into account the developments in the field of textiles at the international level; continuously develops the necessary environment for these researches; educates textile engineers who are experts in the field of textile engineering with up-to-date education plans and who can carry out studies that will transform their knowledge into benefits for society and humanity.
